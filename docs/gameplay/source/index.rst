TrainGame Gameplay
==================

.. contents::
   :local:

You are now responsible for managing a railway company. Your tasks include:

* Building and upgrading train routes. Ensure the correct trains get to the right destinations.
* Purchasing and maintaining trains and tracks.
* Allocating trains to different resources and routes.

For each successful delivery, you earn money which you'll use to expand your railway and keep up with changing demands.

Cities grow, industries evolve, and people need to get to new places. Adapt your strategies to keep your railway company thriving.

Challenges
----------

Trains
~~~~~~

Over time, trains will age and may require upgrades or replacements. New train models with better speed, capacity, and efficiency will become available when you have the money.

Certain engines may be better suited for specific routes or cargo types. Choose the right trains for the job to maximize efficiency and profits.

* Trains do not collide

People
~~~~~~

As cities grow, the demand for passenger transport will increase. Ensure you have enough trains and routes to accommodate the growing population.

Resources
~~~~~~~~~

Different cities will produces different resources, needed by other cities. You're responsible for supplying these resources efficiently. Fail to do this and cities will shrink and eventually die.
